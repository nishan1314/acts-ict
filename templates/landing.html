Core Features

Comprehensive accountability & corruption tracking tools powered by artificial intelligence and data analytics

Smart Detection

AI-powered algorithms analyze procurement data to identify suspicious patterns, unusual bidding behavior, and potential corruption indicators automatically.

Citizen Reporting

Secure and anonymous platform for citizens to report corruption incidents with blockchain-based receipt verification for transparency.

Risk Analytics

Real-time risk scoring and detailed analytics provide insights into corruption patterns across different regions and organizations.

Geographic Mapping

Interactive heatmaps show corruption risk levels across Bangladesh districts, helping target prevention efforts effectively.

Data Protection

Enterprise-grade security ensures all sensitive information is protected while maintaining transparency and accountability.

Public Engagement

Tools for citizens, government officials, and civil society to collaborate in building a more transparent Bangladesh.

How ACTS Works

Simple, effective, and transparent process to fight corruption

1
Data Collection

Government procurement data is automatically collected and processed from various official sources.

2
AI Analysis

Advanced algorithms analyze patterns to detect suspicious activities and calculate corruption risk scores.

3
Citizen Reports

Citizens can securely report corruption incidents with encrypted receipts for verification and tracking.

4
Action & Prevention

Insights drive policy changes and targeted interventions to prevent corruption and improve governance.
